Privacy Policy for Soul Bloom

Last updated: January 25, 2026

Soul Bloom is a student project designed for mental wellness. This application uses Google and Spotify APIs to provide music and health tracking features.

Data Usage

We do not store your personal data on our servers. All authentication is handled directly by Google and Spotify via secure OAuth protocols.

  • Google Fit: Used only to display your step count within the dashboard.
  • YouTube: Used to play music playlists.
  • Spotify: Used to play music playlists.
